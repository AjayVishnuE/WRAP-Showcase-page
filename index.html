HOW WRAP

WORKS?

The Wrap system makes urban environment much cleaner and healthier by accordingly collecting wastes from users door steps. The wrap keeps track of users waste and its impact through proper analysis. At every pickup, user receives a wrap coin as reward. These wrap coins can be collected overtime to claim products and discount coupons.
